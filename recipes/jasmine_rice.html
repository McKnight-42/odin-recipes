Jasmine Rice

Jasmine rice stands out as a simple yet fantastic side dish due to its unique and irreplaceable flavor and smell.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Jasmine rice.
  • 1 to 1 1/4 cups of water.
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt.

Steps

  1. Place rice in a fine-mesh strainer. Rinse rice with cold water until water runs clear. Drain completely.
  2. Add rice, 1 1/4 cups water, and salt to a medium saucepan.
  3. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
  4. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 10 minutes, or until water is absorbed. Remove from heat and let stand, covered, for 10 minutes.
  5. Fluff with a fork before serving.
